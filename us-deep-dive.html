Why Is the US an Outlier?
Structural factors that distinguish the US from peer countries:
‚ùå No Universal Healthcare
~8 million women of reproductive age are uninsured. The US is the only high-income country without universal coverage.
‚ùå Midwife Shortage
Only 4 midwives per 1,000 births vs 58 in Norway. Midwives are underutilized despite evidence of better outcomes.
‚ùå No Paid Leave
The only high-income country with no federally mandated paid maternity leave. Peer countries offer 14+ weeks.
‚ùå Limited Postpartum Care
2/5 women skip their postpartum checkup. Other countries guarantee home visits within a week of birth.
